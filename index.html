Peanut Butter and Jelly

Let's learn how to make a Peanut butter and Jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly
  • 2 slices of Bread

Instructions

  1. Lay out 2 slices of bread.
  2. Spread peanut butter on one slice.
  3. Sprad jam on the other slice.
  4. Stick the two slices together and enjoy!
